PROJECT HOME BY JAYLAXMI FURNISHING

  • Owner's Note

RC-AIRCRAFT PRINTS

  • Canopy, Cowl, Fuselage, Motor Mount, Tray Connectors and Wings are displayed. They were printed in Bowden FDM 3D Printer utilizing PLA filament.

  • The fundamental reason behind this undertaking was to acquire weight stability using PLA filament and gather experience in RC-Aircraft construction.

  • Initially, the project was begun exclusively and will presently be finished by being a part of University RC-Aircraft Team.

3D Printed V8 Engine

  • The functioning model was made in Bowden FDM Printer using PLA filament. The principle thought behind this task was to have better comprehension of the Slicer Settings and the 3D Printing Process to accomplish fine quality parts.

MESH1,FDM 3D PRINTER PROTOTYPE and its parts

  • The video shows that the Mesh1 is printing a Calibration Cube. It was the first part which was made in Mesh1.

  • In the photograph, additional printed parts from Mesh1 are showcased.

MESH1, 3D CAD MODEL

  • MESH1 CAD Model was developed in SolidWorks.

  • The primary thought behind the drive was to convey looks and functionalities of Mesh1 before it was made, to others especially the startup mentors from the Startup Academy.

3D PRINTED TURBOFAN JET ENGINE

  • The working model was made in Bowden FDM Printer using PLA filament.

  • The principle thought behind this task was to have better comprehension of the Slicer Settings and the 3D Printing Process to accomplish fine quality parts.

3D PRINTED AIRBUS A220

  • The static model was made in Bowden FDM Printer using PLA filament.

  • The principle thought behind this task was to have better comprehension of the Slicer Settings and the 3D Printing Process to accomplish fine quality parts.

Password security lock system

  • The video shows the working of a PCB for the door lock system. On entering the corect 4-pin password, LED will glow.

  • In the photograph, the testing circuit of the same project is displayed. It was designed in Autodesk TinkerCad.

  • The primary thought behind this undertaking was to understand the basic principles of password security systems hardware.

Preparatory internship projects

  • The first photograph shows the two sided-tappered cylinder and different types of openings created on a metal piece.

  • The second photograph shows Kicker and cap opener.

PHOTOVOLTAIC TRACKER PROTOTYPE CIRCUIT AND FRAME DRAWING

  • In the first photo, the circuit prototype is shown. Depending upon the light balance, the motor will move in anti- or clockwise direction.

  • In the second photo, the Drawing of the Frame is showcased. The recommended material for the frame was PLA or ABS.

Microcontroller Projects

  • In the first photo, a number counter is shown. It increases or decreases a number on pushing the first or second button individually and pressing both the buttons together would reset the counter.

  • In the second photo, a register was made to display and store the entry and exit time and total number of presents and absents. It even has the Read and Reset data options.

ORIGINAL AND 3D PRINTED TAIL LIGHT

  • It was a Restoration project where the 3D Printed part on the right-side was created to substitute the wrecked Original part on the left-side.

CAR ENGINE CAP 3D MODEL AND ORIGINAL PART

  • It was an another Restoration project where the 3D Printed model of a car engine cap was created.

  • It was used as mould in casting to produce a new car engine cap replacing the destroyed one as shown in the photo.

For over 25 years, Jaylaxmi Furnishing has been a trusted name in the world of premium fabrics, specializing in high-quality curtain and sofa materials. Our journey began with a commitment to deliver exceptional craftsmanship, reliable service, and timeless designs—values that have helped us build long-lasting relationships with our customers.

Today, we are proud to expand our legacy with a new chapter. Jaylaxmi Furnishing is diversifying into the world of home décor manufacturing, bringing to life a wide range of beautifully crafted products that reflect both the elegance of European design and the rich cultural essence of India. Each product is created with a focus on strong durability, aesthetic appeal, and accessibility, ensuring that luxury and style remain within reach for every home.

Our vision is to enrich living spaces with décor that blends functionality with artistry, offering unique pieces that elevate everyday living. As we evolve, we remain dedicated to the trust and quality that have defined us for decades, while embracing innovation to shape the future of home décor.
