Brijesh Shah

For over 25 years, Jaylaxmi Furnishing has been a trusted name in the world of premium fabrics, specializing in high-quality curtain and sofa materials. Our journey began with a commitment to deliver exceptional craftsmanship, reliable service, and timeless designs—values that have helped us build long-lasting relationships with our customers.

Today, we are proud to expand our legacy with a new chapter. Jaylaxmi Furnishing is diversifying into the world of home décor manufacturing, bringing to life a wide range of beautifully crafted products that reflect both the elegance of European design and the rich cultural essence of India. Each product is created with a focus on strong durability, aesthetic appeal, and accessibility, ensuring that luxury and style remain within reach for every home.

Our vision is to enrich living spaces with décor that blends functionality with artistry, offering unique pieces that elevate everyday living. As we evolve, we remain dedicated to the trust and quality that have defined us for decades, while embracing innovation to shape the future of home décor.
